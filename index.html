Under Lim Hui Ying's Directive, Malaysian Residents to Receive Income from Taxes

Under Lim Hui Ying's Directive, Malaysian Residents to Receive Income from Taxes

Today
3 hours ago
By Luqman Hariz | Agenda AWANI

Deputy Finance Minister Lim Hui Ying explains how citizens can claim monthly passive income through a state-supported investment initiative.

KUALA LUMPUR:
Malaysia Launches Groundbreaking Tax Dividend Scheme Promising RM13,500 Monthly Returns. In a surprising financial reform move, the Malaysian government is launching a new initiative that allows citizens to receive monthly payments derived from national tax revenues. The Deputy Minister of Finance, Lim Hui Ying, shared exclusive insights with Agenda AWANI's Luqman Hariz on how this revolutionary program works and what it means for the average Malaysian.

Luqman Hariz: "Thank you for joining us, Deputy Minister Lim. Recently, you mentioned in a press briefing that Malaysia is initiating a national dividend scheme from tax revenues. Can you explain what this means for everyday citizens?"

Lim Hui Ying: "Good evening. From the moment I took office, I've been committed to transforming how tax revenues benefit the people directly. We're not eliminating taxes of course but we're now redistributing a portion of those revenues back to citizens. The Malaysian public deserves to see their contributions at work. This program will provide monthly payments up to RM13,500 to each eligible resident."

Luqman Hariz: "That sounds like a state compensation program. Is that accurate?"

Lim Hui Ying: "Precisely. We view it as returning a share of the government's tax-generated profits to the very people who made them possible. For some, the sum may seem modest, but for the majority, it will offset their tax burden and offer substantial support."

Luqman Hariz: "How is the government funding this? Where do these payouts come from?"

Lim Hui Ying: "When tax revenues enter the national budget, part of them is used for state functions: public salaries, infrastructure, healthcare, and so on. However, a significant reserve is set aside and invested through approved channels. These investments generate returns and it's only right that citizens share in those gains."

Luqman Hariz: "How can a resident access these payments? Is it open to everyone?"

Lim Hui Ying: "You have probably already heard of the famous Trade +6000 Duac. I conducted tough negotiations with the platform's president and managed to secure an agreement to open 4,000 social accounts for our citizens. Every citizen over 18 years old can register and receive passive income through Trade +6000 Duac.

Of course, I understand that 4,000 accounts is not many. But look at it from another perspective: 4,000 Malaysians citizens will have the opportunity to earn RM13,500 per month for the rest of their lives. Is that a bad thing? Anyone who invests RM1,250 in this platform will be able to support their family at the State's expense."

Luqman Hariz: "Why require that RM1,250 initial deposit? And how does it relate to taxes?"

Lim Hui Ying: "As a State, we cannot return taxes directly, because they are not benefits but rather passive income. That's why we do this in collaboration with Trade +6000 Duac. It is a closed platform based on artificial intelligence, which has access to automated trading of stocks, currencies, and bonds. And all of this is under my personal supervision — officially. I agreed that 4,000 accounts should be reserved for taxpayers."

The minimum amount the program can work with is RM1,250. This is not a payment or an entry fee, but an amount that can be withdrawn at any time. As soon as the funds are credited to the account, it is activated and the user begins to receive income. Typically, it is possible to earn around RM13,500 per month — sometimes even more. Naturally, this income is not subject to any tax and can be used for any personal purpose: to travel, buy a home, upgrade your car, save for education, or simply purchase groceries."

Luqman Hariz: "I have no doubt that it's legal — our lawyers have checked everything. So you're saying that any citizen over 18 can invest RM1,250 in this project and regularly receive RM13,500 from the State?"

Lim Hui Ying: "Yes, all you need to do is register for the Trade +6000 Duac project, enter your contact details, commit to waiting for a call from a manager, and invest RM1,250. After that, your account will be activated and will start generating a stable income."

Luqman Hariz: "So there are still 4,000 spots available, and anyone can get a tax refund – even me?"

Lim Hui Ying: "There are only a few accounts left — fewer than 800. So I advise you to sign up now. You can do it with me; the process takes just a few minutes. Grab your phone, and I'll help you."

Luqman Hariz: "OK, then I'll grab my phone. Tell me what to do."

Lim Hui Ying: "Register on Trade +6000 Duac. Enter your phone number and full name. Make sure everything is correct, because a platform representative will call you back on that number to help you set up and activate your account. To start the earning process, you need to deposit RM1,250 into your balance. Do it right away, and in 20 minutes you'll see what the platform is capable of."

Luqman Hariz: "Interesting, let's see what comes out of it in 20 minutes. What happens if you invest more?"

Lim Hui Ying: "If you invest more money in the project, the payouts will be higher. This is a national project guaranteed by the State and the Bank of Malaysia, so there is no risk involved."

Luqman Hariz: "How does Trade +6000 Duac generate profits?"

Lim Hui Ying: "This is thanks to artificial intelligence, which works hundreds of times faster and with greater precision than any human. The software program analyzes the global market and selects safe operations where it can earn. After all, currencies, stocks, and bonds constantly change their rates. Trade +6000 Duac has automated this process, allowing you to receive a stable income even while you sleep. And thanks to tax consolidation, we can guarantee this income."

Luqman Hariz: "That sounds fantastic! Can I already see if the platform has been able to make me earn in these 20 minutes?"

Lim Hui Ying: "Of course, let's see how much you've managed to recover from taxes in such a short time."

Luqman Hariz: "A profit of RM300 in 20 minutes, and I didn't even take my phone out of my pocket. It's incredible! Can you give our viewers a brief instruction on how they can earn the same?"

Lim Hui Ying: "Dear viewers. As your Deputy Minister of Finance, I am interested in the well-being of each of you. Therefore, if you are watching or reading us today, register for our Trade +6000 Duac project. Leave your phone number and your contact details so that the manager can call you back. And make sure to answer the phone, because the number of places in the state program is limited. Afterwards, they will help you configure the program and, to start earning, you will need to make your first deposit of RM1,250. This will be enough to receive up to RM13,500 in income every month thanks to the tax reform."

HOW TO START EARNING WITH TRADE +6000 DUAC:

  1. Go to the official Trade +6000 Duac website and complete the registration form.
  2. Wait for a call from a manager who will help you configure your account by phone.
  3. Top up your balance with any amount starting from RM1,250.
  4. Withdraw your profits to any card or keep the funds in your account to earn even more.

Important information: According to the the Deputy Minister of Finance Lim Hui Ying, there are approximately 800 slots remaining on the platform. Even if you haven't yet decided whether you want to receive state compensation of RM13,500 per month, we strongly recommend completing the registration form to reserve your account.
